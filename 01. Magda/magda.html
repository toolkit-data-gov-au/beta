Need support? Contact us

Data.gov.au is now supported by the Australian Bureau of Statistics.

If you require assistance, please contact us on support@data.gov.au and allow for up to 5 business days turn around.

To allow us to streamline these enquiries and respond as soon as possible, please only contact us using the email above, otherwise your enquiries may be missed.

New user account registrations are temporarily unavailable. If you intend to register as a new user, please contact support@data.gov.au for assistance.
